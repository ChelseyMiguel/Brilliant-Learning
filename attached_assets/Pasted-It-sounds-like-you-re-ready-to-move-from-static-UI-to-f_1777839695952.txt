## 🛠️ Troubleshooting: Why aren't they showing?

If your animations aren't appearing, check these three things in your code:
1.  **Missing Libraries:** Ensure you have `framer-motion` or `lucide-react` installed in your project.
2.  **Z-Index:** Sometimes the "Slate-50" background can sit on top of the animation. Ensure your SVG or Canvas has a higher `z-index`.
3.  **State Mismatch:** If the animation is tied to `isCorrect`, make sure that state is actually changing when you click the button.
