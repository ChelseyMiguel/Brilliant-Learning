## 3. Technical Checklist: Why is it "Not Working"?

If you've pasted code and the button still does nothing, check these three **"Silent Killers"**:

| Issue | What to check |
| :--- | :--- |
| **The "Z-Index" Trap** | Is there an invisible "overlay" or "div" sitting on top of your buttons? Try adding `className="relative z-50"` to your button. |
| **The "State Shadow"** | Are you updating the state (e.g., `setSelected`) but the button is looking at a different variable? Ensure your button's `onClick` is calling the exact function you defined. |
| **Animation Blocking** | If you are using **Framer Motion**, sometimes a `layout` transition can move a button visually, but its "clickable area" stays in the old spot. Try removing `layout` temporarily to see if it fixes the click. |
